# Product Goals

1. **Achieve Zero-Friction Onboarding:** Enable new users to fully configure their digital store, inventory, and supplier network in under 3 minutes simply by uploading photos of existing documents (The Resurrection).
2. **Drive High Automation Trust:** Successfully guide users through the "Autonomy Ladder," converting manual approvers to autonomous operations by consistently providing accurate, risk-free recommendations.
3. **Ensure Fail-Safe AI Execution:** Maintain a 100% reliable safety net for autonomous actions, ensuring all actions can be reversed within 1 hour, are hard-capped, and easily revocable.
4. **Expand Horizontal Utility:** Provide scalable, domain-specific playbooks (e.g., Healthcare, Retail, Manufacturing) that allow businesses outside of simple retail to leverage the Otto workflow engine for tasks like document generation and multi-department approvals.
5. **High Accuracy Data Extraction:** Maintain near-perfect accuracy in vision extraction (LLM schema-locked via Zod) to prevent hallucinated data from corrupting the ledger or inventory.
