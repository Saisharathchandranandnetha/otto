# Problem Statement

Small businesses operate on razor-thin margins and limited time. The core problems they face in daily operations are:

1. **High Friction in Digitization:** Migrating from paper ledgers, printed invoices, and WhatsApp chats to a digital ERP or inventory management system requires days of manual data entry. Most small business owners abandon software before experiencing its value due to this setup cost.
2. **Operational Bottlenecks:** Routine tasks like tracking inventory, reordering from suppliers, and managing multi-department approvals consume significant time that could be spent on growth and customer service.
3. **Lack of Trust in Automation:** Existing automation tools are either too rigid or operate as "black boxes." Business owners fear losing control over spending and critical communications if they let software make autonomous decisions.
4. **Fragmented Workflows:** Solutions are often siloed, requiring different tools for document generation, customer support, and inventory reordering, leading to operational chaos.
