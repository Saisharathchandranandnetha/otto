# Value Proposition

**Otto: The software that installs itself in 3 minutes, then earns your trust like a real employee.**

- **Zero-Typing Setup:** Go from a shoebox of paper receipts and WhatsApp chats to a fully functioning digital backend in 3 minutes via our "Resurrection" vision-extraction engine.
- **Intelligent Autonomous Reordering:** Stop tracking inventory manually. Otto predicts reorder points and handles purchase orders, initially asking for your approval and eventually doing it autonomously once it earns your trust.
- **Fail-Safe Autonomy:** Relax knowing that every autonomous action Otto takes is capped, logged, reversible for 1 hour, and revocable instantly with a single toggle.
- **Industry Versatility:** Whether you run a retail shop, a manufacturing plant, or a legal practice, Otto’s domain playbooks adapt to your specific workflows (approvals, document generation, support responses) without complex configuration.
