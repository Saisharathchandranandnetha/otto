# Business Requirements

1. **Target Audience:** Small to medium businesses (SMBs) struggling with analog data (paper, WhatsApp) and manual inventory/process management.
2. **Market Differentiation:** Must differentiate from traditional ERPs by removing data-entry friction and focusing on "earned" AI autonomy rather than rigid rule-based automation.
3. **Safety & Compliance:** The business requires that the application never execute destructive or financially binding actions without explicit initial permission, and must support strict undo/caps to protect the user's finances.
4. **Platform Availability:** Must be accessible via a standard web browser (desktop/mobile) as a Next.js application, with the ability to communicate with customers/suppliers via WhatsApp (Twilio integration).
5. **Scalability Strategy:** The underlying engine must be vertically agnostic, allowing expansion from basic Retail (reordering) to other industries (HR, Legal, Manufacturing) via pre-built domain playbooks.
