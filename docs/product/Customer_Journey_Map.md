# Customer Journey Map

## Phase 1: Discovery & Skepticism
- **Touchpoint:** Hears about Otto as "software that installs in 3 minutes."
- **Customer Emotion:** Skeptical. They've tried ERPs before and failed due to data entry.
- **Action:** Visits the website and decides to try the demo.

## Phase 2: "The Resurrection" (Aha! Moment)
- **Touchpoint:** Uploads a photo of a messy, handwritten invoice to the `/resurrection` endpoint.
- **Customer Emotion:** Amazement. Watches the live UI narrate the extraction of line items, prices, and suppliers perfectly.
- **Action:** Clicks "Confirm" and instantly possesses a populated digital ledger.

## Phase 3: Supervised Operation
- **Touchpoint:** Checks the Approval Feed daily.
- **Customer Emotion:** Confident. Otto suggests reordering items running low, but the user must click "Approve." Nothing happens without their say-so.
- **Action:** Approves the same reorder task for the 3rd time.

## Phase 4: Granting Autonomy (The Autonomy Ladder)
- **Touchpoint:** Otto prompts: "You've approved this 3 times. Would you like me to handle this automatically in the future?"
- **Customer Emotion:** Trusting but cautious. The safety nets (1-hour undo, spending caps) reassure them.
- **Action:** Clicks "Promote."

## Phase 5: Scaled Operations
- **Touchpoint:** Opens the Autonomous Workflow Agents console to explore other playbooks.
- **Customer Emotion:** Empowered. They realize Otto can handle document generation and customer support, not just inventory.
- **Action:** Stages new workflows for other parts of their business.
