# Product Strategy

Our strategy for Otto centers on **trust-driven automation** and **frictionless onboarding** for small businesses.

## 1. Frictionless Onboarding ("The Resurrection")
Small businesses typically fail to adopt new software because of the steep data entry and setup cost. Our strategy eliminates this barrier by allowing users to upload unstructured, messy physical artifacts (photos of invoices, handwritten ledgers, WhatsApp chat exports). Otto uses vision extraction and entity resolution to automatically build the digital business state in 3 minutes.

## 2. Earned Autonomy ("The Autonomy Ladder")
Trust is the biggest barrier to AI adoption in business operations. Otto requires human approval for all actions initially. As the user approves actions (e.g., 3 consecutive reorder approvals), Otto explicitly asks for a "promotion" to execute these tasks autonomously. 
Safety is guaranteed through:
- Hard caps on actions/spending.
- Comprehensive logging.
- 1-hour reversibility (undo).
- One-toggle revocation.

## 3. Extensibility via Domain Engines
Once trust and data ingestion are solved, Otto scales horizontally. We use a deterministic playbook model to adapt Otto's autonomous workflow engine to multiple verticals: Education, Healthcare, HR, Legal, Manufacturing, Sales, Customer Support, and Retail. This transforms Otto from a simple reordering tool into a comprehensive horizontal AI operator.
