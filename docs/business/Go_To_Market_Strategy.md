# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy: Otto

## 1. Launch Phasing

### Phase 1: Niche Beachhead (Retail & Local Manufacturing)
- **Target Persona:** Independent retail stores (e.g., boutique apparel like "Priya's Fashion") and small-scale manufacturers.
- **Why:** High volume of paper invoices, frequent reordering needs, clear ROI on inventory management.
- **Action:** Launch the core "Resurrection" feature heavily targeting this demographic. 

### Phase 2: Horizontal Expansion via Domain Playbooks
- **Target Persona:** Clinics, Legal Practices, HR agencies.
- **Action:** Roll out the MVP domain-specific playbooks (Healthcare, Legal, HR) built on the core trust engine. Cross-pollinate learnings from the retail beachhead.

### Phase 3: B2B Network Effects
- **Target Persona:** Suppliers and vendors of Phase 1 & 2 users.
- **Action:** Leverage Otto's auto-generated PDF Purchase Orders sent via WhatsApp. Include a subtle CTA: *"This PO was generated by Otto. Get an AI operator for your business."*

## 2. Acquisition Channels

**1. Product-Led Virality (The "Wow" Demo):**
- Create short-form video content (TikTok, Reels, LinkedIn) demonstrating "The Resurrection." Showing a messy pile of receipts turning into a digital dashboard in 60 seconds is highly shareable and viscerally appeals to stressed business owners.

**2. SMB Aggregators & Partnerships:**
- Partner with local accounting firms. Accountants hate dealing with their clients' shoeboxes of receipts. Offer Otto to accountants as a tool to digitize their clients instantly, effectively making accountants our distribution channel.

**3. WhatsApp Ecosystem:**
- Utilize WhatsApp Business marketing. Since SMBs live on WhatsApp, reaching them via targeted campaigns on the platform they use for operations ensures high visibility.

## 3. Onboarding & Activation Strategy
- **Zero-Friction Entry:** No credit card required. The user's first action is simply taking a picture with their phone. 
- **The "Aha!" Moment:** Occurs when the user taps "This is my business ✅" and sees their physical ledger transformed into a dashboard.
- **Nurturing Trust:** In-app tooltips guide the user through their first 3 manual approvals, explicitly explaining how the "Autonomy Ladder" works to set expectations.

## 4. Positioning & Messaging
- **Tagline:** *"Hire your first AI employee."*
- **Narrative:** Shift the conversation away from "software you have to learn" to "an assistant that learns your business." Emphasize safety (caps, undo buttons) to proactively address AI skepticism.
