# Business Model Canvas: Otto

## 1. Key Partners
- **LLM Providers:** OpenRouter, OpenAI (GPT-4o), Google (Gemini) for core extraction and reasoning.
- **Infrastructure:** Vercel (Hosting), Supabase (Database), Docker.
- **Integration Partners:** Twilio (WhatsApp API).
- **Distribution Partners:** Local accounting firms, SMB consultants, business associations.

## 2. Key Activities
- **Platform Development:** Maintaining the Agent Core (machine, gate, trust mechanisms) and UI.
- **AI Optimization:** Refining prompts, Zod schemas, and vision extraction accuracy.
- **Customer Onboarding:** Facilitating "The Resurrection" process for new users.
- **Security & Compliance:** Ensuring data privacy, transaction safety, and audit logs.

## 3. Key Resources
- **Proprietary Architecture:** The deterministic Agent State Machine and Trust Engine.
- **Engineering Team:** AI, Backend, and Frontend talent.
- **Data:** Anonymized extraction patterns to improve schema and prompt robustness.

## 4. Value Propositions
- **For Traditional SMBs:** "A digital business in 3 minutes, zero typing." Digitize paper ledgers and invoices instantly.
- **For Business Owners:** An AI employee that earns trust. Starts by drafting and asking for approval, graduates to autonomous execution with safety caps and undo capabilities.
- **For Operations:** Consolidates multi-channel data (paper, WhatsApp) into a single, actionable dashboard.

## 5. Customer Relationships
- **Co-Pilot / Assistant:** Otto acts as an intelligent employee, constantly seeking feedback and approval until trust is established.
- **Self-Serve:** Low-touch onboarding via the camera/upload interface.
- **Transparent & Auditable:** Builds trust through visible confidence scores and a robust activity trace.

## 6. Channels
- **Digital Marketing:** Targeted ads demonstrating the 3-minute "Shoebox" onboarding.
- **Product-Led Growth:** Suppliers receiving WhatsApp POs from Otto may adopt the software themselves.
- **App Stores & Integrations:** Shopify, WhatsApp Business directory.

## 7. Customer Segments
- **Micro & Small Businesses:** Retailers (e.g., boutique fashion), local manufacturing, healthcare clinics.
- **Tech-Lagging Sectors:** Businesses currently running on paper, physical ledgers, and disjointed WhatsApp chats.
- **Time-Poor Solopreneurs:** Owners who cannot afford dedicated administrative staff.

## 8. Cost Structure
- **LLM API Costs:** Variable costs based on vision extraction volume and agent reasoning loops.
- **Hosting & Infrastructure:** Database, serverless functions, and storage for document photos.
- **R&D / Payroll:** Continuous improvement of the AI core and domain playbooks.
- **Marketing & Sales:** Customer acquisition costs (CAC).

## 9. Revenue Streams
- **SaaS Subscription:** Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) for platform access.
- **Usage-Based Pricing:** Tiered limits on the number of documents processed or autonomous actions executed per month.
- **Add-Ons:** Premium domains/playbooks, advanced WhatsApp integration, or higher autonomy spending caps.
