# Pricing Strategy: Otto

## 1. Pricing Philosophy
Otto's pricing must reflect its core value proposition: drastically reducing manual labor and earning trust over time. The strategy utilizes a "Land and Expand" approach. The initial friction to use the product must be zero, matching the 3-minute onboarding promise. Monetization scales only when the business owner explicitly grants the AI more responsibility.

## 2. Tiered Structure

### 1. The "Shoebox" Tier (Free / Freemium)
- **Target:** Skeptical SMB owners who want to test the "digital business in 3 minutes" claim.
- **Features:** 
  - The Resurrection pipeline (up to 50 documents/month).
  - Manual approval feed (infinite).
  - Basic analytics and ledger views.
- **Value Proposition:** Replaces basic spreadsheets and manual entry for free.
- **Goal:** Drive user acquisition and demonstrate AI reliability.

### 2. The "Co-Pilot" Tier (e.g., $29/month)
- **Target:** Growing SMBs that need efficiency.
- **Features:**
  - Up to 250 documents/month.
  - Unlocks the **Autonomy Ladder**: Otto can ask for promotion.
  - Up to 100 autonomous actions/month included.
  - WhatsApp integration for basic alerting.
- **Value Proposition:** The AI transitions from a tool you use to an employee you manage.

### 3. The "Operator" Tier (e.g., $99/month + Usage)
- **Target:** Established SMBs scaling operations (e.g., Retail, Manufacturing).
- **Features:**
  - Unlimited document ingestion (fair use).
  - Unlimited autonomous actions (billed via usage overage).
  - Advanced domain playbooks (Healthcare, Legal, HR).
  - Custom autonomy caps (e.g., "Auto-approve up to $50,000").
  - 1-hour undo windows and advanced audit trails.

## 3. Usage Overage Pricing
For users on the Co-Pilot or Operator tiers, exceeding the baseline autonomous actions incurs a micro-fee per action (e.g., $0.10 per autonomous reorder PO generated and sent). 
*Psychology:* The business owner only pays this fee when they have explicitly permitted Otto to act independently, directly correlating the cost with time saved.

## 4. Discounting & Promotions
- **The "Trust Milestone" Discount:** Offer a one-time credit when a user approves their first autonomous graduation, incentivizing the transition from manual to automated workflows.
