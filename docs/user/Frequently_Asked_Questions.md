# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can Otto execute actions without my permission?**
A: By default, no. Every action is human-gated initially. Only after repeated approvals (usually 3 times) will Otto ask for permission to act autonomously.

**Q: What happens if Otto makes a mistake while acting autonomously?**
A: Autonomous actions are capped and logged. Most actions can be cleanly reversed within a 1-hour window.

**Q: Does Otto support my specific industry?**
A: Otto includes MVP playbooks for Education, Healthcare, HR, Legal, Manufacturing, Sales, Customer Support, and Retail.
